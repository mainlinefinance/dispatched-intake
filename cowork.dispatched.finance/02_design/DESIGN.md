## Do-not-ship checklist (from brief, Section 3)
- Canvas: `#FAF7F2` warm off-white (never pure white).
- Primary: steel-blue `#1F4561`.
- Accent: hi-viz orange `#E67A1F` — CTAs and focus states only.
- Typography: Archivo (display), IBM Plex Sans (body), IBM Plex Mono (all numeric + monetary values).
- Corners: 4 / 8 / 12 / 20px max. Never pill-shaped.
- Banned: blue→purple gradients, Inter, Poppins.
- Anti-references: MCA broker sites, generic YC fintech, telehealth sterility, consumer neobanks.
