# Compliance Plan

The running plan tying legal/regulatory workstreams to the build and launch timeline. Updated as lawyer engagements land and filings progress.

> **Status:** to draft. Pairs with `/04_compliance/state_licensing_tracker.md` (tactical) and the `udaap_review_notes.md` (copy-level findings).

## Workstreams

1. **Outside counsel selection.** 3 candidates this week. See `/04_compliance/lawyer_candidates/`.
2. **State commercial-lending-broker licensing.** CA, NY, VA, UT first. Track filings, costs, and lead times in `state_licensing_tracker.md`.
3. **Disclosure documents.** Privacy policy, ToS, cookie policy. FCRA/GLBA/ECOA-aware. 4–6 week cycle with outside counsel.
4. **UDAAP discipline.** Copy review on every new landing/geo page before publish. Quarterly refresh on APR + timing pages.
5. **TCPA + 10DLC.** SMS consent language in Screen 8 + opt-in flow. 10DLC campaign registration 2–4 weeks through Twilio.
6. **Insurance.** E&O + cyber. Bind before first routed application.
7. **Lender DPAs.** Data processing addendum signed with each panel lender before go-live.

## Target horizon

- **+2 wks:** counsel engaged, CA + NY filings initiated.
- **+6 wks:** privacy policy + ToS live, 10DLC approved, E&O bound.
- **+12 wks:** all 4 launch states (CA/NY/VA/UT) licensed or provisional, first lender DPA executed.

## Why this document exists
The brief calls compliance the most underbudgeted section of the plan. This file forces it to stay visible alongside the build, not behind it.
