# Paid Test Plan

Paid is calibration, not scale (brief Section 5).

## Window
Week 4 post-launch. $500–2K/month test budget.

## Target CAC
**< $1,500.** That's ~50% of expected revenue per funded loan ($3,000 at 3% of $100K avg principal).

## Keyword focus
- Owner-operator repair loans
- Trucking working capital
- Trucking equipment financing

## Geo scope
Metros in the first 10 launched states only: **TX, CA, FL, GA, OH, IL, NY, PA, WA, AZ.**

## Decision point (Section 10, item 5 — founder rule)
**Write down the dollar ceiling that can be lost without changing runway math before week 4 opens.** Do not make that decision at 11 PM on a Thursday staring at a dashboard.

Ceiling: __________ (fill before paid starts)

## Measurement
- Report CAC weekly via PostHog funnel → submit → (post-integration) funded.
- Report per-keyword CAC after 14 days of spend.
- Kill any keyword with CAC > 2× target after 14 days.

## Landing match
All paid traffic lands on the flagship `/trucking` or a geo page matching the keyword — never the homepage (until `/` replaces `/trucking`).
